Information Technologu

Information Technology (IT) at GNDEC, led by Dr. Kulvinder Singh Mann, focuses on the practical application of computing technologies in business and industry, covering areas like web development, database management, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. The department offers a dynamic learning environment with well-maintained labs, project-based learning, and regular skill enhancement workshops. IT students benefit from shared placement opportunities with CSE and are recruited by companies like Wipro, Capgemini, and Cognizant. With an industry-oriented curriculum and growing demand for IT professionals, the program equips students for careers in software services, systems administration, and emerging tech fields.
