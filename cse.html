Computer Science & Engineering

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at GNDEC Ludhiana is one of the most prominent and competitive programs, offering in-depth knowledge in programming, algorithms, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and software engineering. Headed by Dr. Kiran Jyoti, the department is well-equipped with modern labs, experienced faculty, and a curriculum aligned with industry needs. Students frequently participate in coding competitions, internships, and projects, with strong placement support from top recruiters like TCS, Infosys, and Amazon. The CSE program emphasizes both theoretical depth and hands-on problem-solving, producing graduates well-prepared for roles in software development, data science, and tech research.
