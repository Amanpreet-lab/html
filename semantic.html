About GNDEC

Established in 1956,Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College(GNDEC),Ludhiana is one of the oldest and most prestigious engineering instutions in North India.The college is managed by Nankana Sahib Education Trust and is affiliated with Punjab Technical University.

Departments

Computer Science & Engineering

Offers B.tech, M.tech and Ph.D. programs focused on software,AI,cybersecurity and data science.

Mechnical Engineering

One of the core departments with a strong emphasis on practical knowledge and research projects.

Civil Engineering

Know for structural and environmental engineering expertise,with advanced labs field training.

Admissions

Admissions are based on JEE Main for UG courses ans GATE for PG programs.Reservations policies as per government norms are followed.
